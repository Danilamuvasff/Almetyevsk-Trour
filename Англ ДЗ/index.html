Where to go in Almetyevsk?

Almetyevsk is a modern and dynamically developing city offering many interesting places to visit:

Shamsinur Park

Shamsinur Park is a center of spiritual and physical recreation for citizens. It is located in the residential neighborhood 2 "B" in the city center, on the territory of secondary school No. 11.

The main facilities of the park are running and walking paths (1.7 and 2.3 kilometers, respectively).

The park territory is surrounded by greenery: more than 500 large-sized seedlings have been planted, including cherries, cherry trees and apple trees. The central alley is decorated with several dozen flower beds, lanterns and benches made in the same style.

Address: Mira street, 13.

Museum of the History of the city of Almetyevsk

The Almetyevsk Museum of Local Lore is dedicated to the history and culture of the Almetyevsk region. It was founded in 1992 and opened on May 18, 1994.

At the Museum of the History of Almetyevsk, you will learn how a small village turned into a prosperous modern city thanks to the discovery and development of oil fields.

The museum pays special attention to the history of the oil industry: you will see unique exhibits related to oil production and refining, learn about people who have made a huge contribution to the development of this industry, and understand how oil has changed the face and fate of Almetyevsk.

Visiting the Museum of the History of Almetyevsk is an opportunity to touch the history and culture of a unique region, to get acquainted with its heroes and achievements.

Working hours: daily from 10:00 to 16:00, Saturday — by request, Sunday — day off.

City beach

The beach on the city lake of Almetyevsk was created in July 2016; the beach improvement works were carried out as part of the Year of Water Protection Zones in the republic. There is a wake park with two trails for extreme water sports. Sports equipment, a climbing wall, a rope park, volleyball and children's playgrounds, a boat and catamaran rental center, a fitness and yoga pavilion, a large outdoor water complex with a swimming pool, sauna and a spray park - a children's water playground — have appeared in the active recreation area. Professional instructors with international qualifications work with adults, and children are entertained by animators.

Beach Features:

  • There are changing rooms, showers, toilets, drinking fountains;
  • There is a large playground with climbing walls and exercise equipment for children.;
  • Outdoor swimming pool and climbing wall;
  • Boat and catamaran rental;
  • Fitness and yoga pavilion;

Professional instructors with international qualifications and children's animators work on the beach. A permanent post of medical workers and rescuers is equipped. 3 In summer, the Karakuz Festival is held here with free concerts by Russian pop stars.

Cascade of ponds

A single water-architectural complex with a comfortable walking area. It is located in the central part of the city, on Beloglazova St., on the site between the central mosque on Marjani St. and the bath complex, between Beloglazova, Neftyanikov, Gafiatullin streets.

The first stage of the cascade of ponds was inaugurated on August 27, 2003. The swampy ravine that ran between the city lake and Lenin Street turned into a clean, level pond with well-equipped shores, pedestrian paths, greenery, and lighting. The highlight was the delicate bridge, popularly called the "Bridge of Lovers."

In 2013, the seventh stage of the cascade of ponds was inaugurated, completing a single water-architectural complex. In the same year, Cascade Ponds received the State Prize of the Republic of Tatarstan in the field of science and technology for the creation of unique memorial and cultural facilities in Almetyevsk.

Today, there are small ponds with bridges in the center of the park. Nearby is Tatarstan's largest playground with an area of 5,000 square meters. According to the idea of the designers, the public space is divided into zones, each of which is associated with one of the elements: earth, air, water. There are swans and ducks swimming in the pond, which can be fed. In the evening, the park is beautifully illuminated, which makes it an ideal place for walking.

Karakuz Square

This is a public space with an area of 2.5 hectares, which was landscaped in 2018-2020. The purpose of the landscaping is to combine the various functions of the Tatneft Company's business center, create a high—quality urban environment and a center for cultural activity.

The layout of the square is subordinated to the main directions of pedestrian movement and is centered around the sculpture "Karakuz" by Dasha Namdakov. Behind the sculpture is a "forest area" with an eco-trail that leads from the office and administrative complex to the square in front of the Almet Community Center, designed for urban events.
